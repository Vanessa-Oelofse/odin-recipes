Broccoli Cheese Soup

This broccoli and cheese soup recipe is just FILLED with all the good stuff.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Gnocchi
  • Carrots
  • Chicken Broth
  • White Wine
  • Half and Half

Directions

  1. To begin, cut the broccoli into bite-sized florets, and then blanch the broccoli (check the recipe card for details on how to do this).
  2. Next, cook the gnocchi according to package instructions, drain, rinse, and set aside.
  3. Heat butter and oil in a dutch oven or large pot. Then add in the carrots, onion, and fennel, and cook for 4 minutes. Add in the garlic and cook another 30 seconds.
  4. Sprinkle flour over the vegetables, then cook until the mixture starts to brown. Then gradually pour in the chicken broth.
  5. Stir in the blanched broccoli, and then whisk in the wine and half-and-half. Then add in the red pepper flakes and bay leaf.
  6. Increase the heat and bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat again and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  7. Once the vegetables have softened, stir in the cooked gnocchi.
  8. Remove soup from heat and stir in the cheese.
